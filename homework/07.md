## Homework 7 - Rank and Nullity

**_Due {{ page.date }}_**. 


1. Find the nullity and rank of the matrix 
$$
    A = \begin{pmatrix} 
    8 & -1 & 3 & 3 \\
    3 & 0 & 1 & -1 \\
    2 & -1 & 1 & 5 
    \end{pmatrix}
$$

2. Find the rank and nullity of $A^T$.   

3. Suppose we have $A = BC$. Show that 
$$
    \operatorname{rank} A \leq \operatorname{rank} B 
$$
